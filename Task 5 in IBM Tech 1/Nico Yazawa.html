Nico Yazawa

Normally, Nico has quite an abrasive personality, but when she takes on her alternate
personality,she becomes a happy,upbeat character who is always smiling,often saying
her catchphrase,"Nico Nico Nii!" (にっこにっこにー), accompanied with a pose.
She usually thinks a bit highly of herself but, despite that, she is also caring.
